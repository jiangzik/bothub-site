# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 8, 2026

BotHub ("we", "us", "our") values your privacy. This policy applies to BotHub apps on Android, iOS, and desktop, as well as the official website.

## 1. Information we collect (minimum necessary)

We process information only as needed to provide login and connection features:

1. Basic account information: data you provide when registering or signing in (for example username, nickname, avatar, email, or phone number, depending on your login method).
2. Account security information: data used for identity verification and session management (for example login status, token validity, and device login records).
3. Connection and runtime logs: technical data required to support relay/direct connections and service reliability (for example IP address, connection time, connection status, error codes, and device/network environment data).

We do not collect or store your chat message body, attachment content, or third-party API keys for operational purposes.

## 2. Why we use this information

We use the information above only to:

1. Create and manage accounts and complete authentication.
2. Establish and maintain relay/direct connections.
3. Protect service security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
4. Comply with legal obligations.

## 3. Local data and third-party services

1. BotHub follows a local-first model, and your conversations/settings remain on your device by default.
2. When you use model or network features, requests are sent from your device to third-party providers you choose or configure.
3. Data processing by those third-party providers is governed by their own privacy policies.

## 4. Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share necessary data only when:

1. Sharing is required with infrastructure providers to deliver core functionality.
2. Required by law, regulation, court order, or government request.
3. Needed in emergencies to protect the life, property, or safety of users or the public.

If a merger, split, or asset transfer occurs, we will require the successor entity to continue protecting your data under this policy.

## 5. Data retention

1. Basic account information: retained until account deletion or service termination, then deleted or anonymized unless longer retention is legally required.
2. Connection/runtime logs: retained only for the period needed for functionality and security auditing, in principle no more than 30 days unless law requires otherwise.
3. Local data stored on your device is managed and deleted by you.

## 6. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may:

1. Access and correct your basic account information.
2. Delete your account and request deletion of related personal information.
3. Withdraw consent (this does not affect processing based on consent before withdrawal).
4. Contact us for policy explanations or complaints.

If you request account deletion in-app, we will process it after identity verification.

## 7. Mobile permissions

On Android/iOS, BotHub may request permissions only for features you actively use:

1. Camera access: capture photos for messages.
2. Photo library/storage access: choose and import media or files.
3. Microphone access: voice input when enabled.

You can revoke permissions at any time in system settings, but related features may stop working.

## 8. Cross-border transfers

If cross-border data transfer occurs due to infrastructure deployment or service providers, we will comply with applicable legal requirements and apply safeguards such as encryption and access controls.

## 9. Children

If you are under the age required to provide valid consent in your jurisdiction, use BotHub under parent/guardian supervision.

## 10. Policy updates

We may update this policy based on product or regulatory changes. The latest version will be published on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
