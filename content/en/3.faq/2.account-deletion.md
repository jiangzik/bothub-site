# Account Deletion

Last updated: March 18, 2026

This page explains how to delete your BotHub cloud account and related cloud data.

## 1. In-app deletion

1. Open BotHub.
2. Go to `Settings` -> `Account Login`.
3. Tap `Delete account` and confirm.

## 2. Web self-service deletion

Use the web form below to request deletion with your account email and verification code.

<account-deletion-form lang="en" />

## 3. Processing time

After verification, deletion is typically completed within 15 business days.

## 4. Data deleted after account deletion

After deletion is completed, cloud-side account data is removed from active systems, including:

1. Account profile and authentication identities.
2. Active and historical sign-in sessions.
3. Registered cloud devices and sync state.
4. Cloud sync records associated with the account.
5. Attachment/avatar references associated with the account.

## 5. Data that may be retained

Some records may be retained for a limited period when required for legal compliance, dispute handling, or security investigations.

## 6. Local device data

Account deletion affects cloud-side data. Data stored locally on your own device is managed by you.
